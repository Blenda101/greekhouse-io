Feature Spotlight

Homecoming.
Both houses.

The undergrad chapter runs their weekend. The alumni association runs their own reunion alongside it. Two separate GreekHouse accounts. Two separate programs. And when they choose to collaborate — they can.

ℹ Homecoming belongs to undergraduate chapters and their affiliated alumni associations. Graduate chapters are city-based civic organizations with their own year-round calendar — independent of campus homecoming.
01
Separate accounts, same weekend
Each org manages their own events, registrations, and finances independently. No shared access unless you choose it.
02
Three event tiers per org
Public (campus & community), member-only (verified actives or alumni), and private (officers, invited guests).
03
Event P&L tracked automatically
Every ticket sale and expense logged against the event. Close of weekend, both treasurers have a clean financial picture.
04
Optional collaboration
When a joint event makes sense, both orgs can cross-promote or co-host. Collaboration is a choice, not a default.
