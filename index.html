How it works

Three steps.
That's it.

🏕️
01
Set up your reunion

Create your reunion space in minutes. Add your dates, location, and events. Open registration with custom forms — meals, t-shirts, travel details, whatever you need.

📨
02
Invite everyone

Share one link. People register, pay, and get everything they need in one place. No chasing RSVPs. No group text chaos. No spreadsheet juggling.

Run the weekend from your phone. Check people in, share updates, collect last-minute payments. After it's over, your reunion is saved — ready for next time.
